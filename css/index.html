About Me

I'm a bit of a rebel without a cause and a free-spirited wild child with a fierce streak of Type A pulsing through my veins. A forever student with a wanderlust for spending time in faraway places learning all I can about cultures, history, and people. My guilty pleasures are true crime, biographies, and documentaries. If you see me on the train, I'm most likely listening to a podcast or reading while trying to avoid the unwelcome cell phone conversations of the truly oblivious.

Currently, my mentors in coding are Colt Steele, Traversy Media, and freeCodeCamp. My mentors-of-the-moment in life are Brendon Bruchard, Michelle Obama, and the writings of Charles Bukowski (just to provide an interesting perspective). My mentors tend to change because I deeply believe that one must follow their own heart, mind, and intuition first and foremost.

My copilot in life is my dog Murphy, otherwise known as 'The M Dog', 'Murphy Girl', or just plain 'Murph', and my biggest fans are my three kids; Noah, AnnaVi, and Addison. They're my heart and soul, the best of this life, and the force behind my passion for spreading bits of good in the world.
